## Awards
The top three teams in each track will receive prizes based on the final rankings. Award-winning teams will also be invited to present their research at the ICASSP 2026 special session and workshop.

| Rank | Prize |
| --- | --- |
| 1st Place | USD 5,000 |
| 2nd Place | USD 3,000 |
